Bio

Guns N' Roses is an American rock band formed in 1985 in Los Angeles, California. You make recognize Guns N Roses by 3 of their most popular songs "Welcome to the Jungle", "Paradise City", "Sweet Child o' Mine". Guns N' Roses produced 6 studio albums in total. The band is still touring today with new shows set for Summer 2021.
